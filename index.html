Software, Subscriptions & Account Management

  • Client-Owned Subscriptions: For all third-party cloud services, including Zapier and Make, the client is required to create and pay for their own accounts. We will guide them on which plan to purchase and they will provide us with secure, delegated access.
  • Agency-Managed Infrastructure (Hosting & Tier 3): For website clients, we provide hosting through our professional Reseller Hosting account. Each client receives a dedicated, isolated cPanel account for security and control. For Tier 3 automation projects, the workflows run on our self-hosted n8n instance, and the cost is included in the monthly management fee.

Support, Maintenance & Warranty

  • Initial Warranty Period: All new builds include a 90-day warranty for fixing any bugs or errors related to the original scope of work at no charge.
  • Service Level Agreement (SLA) for Monthly Plans: Our commitment to responsiveness is a premium feature of our service.
Severity Level Description Target First Response Time
1 - Critical Entire website is down; Core business automation is failing. 2 Business Hours
2 - High A key feature is broken; A major workflow is producing errors. 8 Business Hours
3 - Medium Minor feature malfunction; content display issue. 1 Business Day

A "response" is an acknowledgment of the issue and the start of an investigation. Full "resolution" time is dependent on the underlying service provider (e.Example: Zapier, Make, hosting provider). We will manage the issue and leverage our provider support channels to expedite resolution on your behalf.

Strategic Client Management & Growth

Our goal is to be a long-term strategic partner, consistently identifying opportunities for optimization and growth within your business.

Scenario 1: Platform Optimization Project

  • Trigger: A client's Zapier bill increases significantly due to high volume.
  • Our Action: We proactively propose a fixed-price project to migrate workflows to the more cost-effective Make platform, backed by a clear ROI analysis.
  • Outcome: Client saves money on software, and our agency provides high-value strategic service, strengthening our partnership.

Scenario 2: Advanced Solution Upgrade

  • Trigger: Client expresses a new, complex operational challenge unaddressable by current setup.
  • Our Action: We schedule a discovery to scope a custom solution using our self-hosted n8n platform.
  • Outcome: Client gains a Tier 3 project, enhancing efficiency and increasing our recurring revenue.
